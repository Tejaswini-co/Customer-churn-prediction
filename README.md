# Customer Churn Prediction: A Complete Data Science Journey

## About This Project

Welcome to my comprehensive customer churn prediction project! This isn't just another machine learning exercise—it's a real-world demonstration of how data science can solve critical business problems in the telecommunications industry.

Imagine you're running a telecom company and every month, valuable customers are slipping away to competitors. This project shows exactly how to identify who's at risk of leaving and what you can do about it.

## The Business Challenge

Customer churn is the silent killer of telecom businesses. When customers leave:
- You lose recurring revenue streams
- Acquiring new customers costs 5-25x more than keeping existing ones
- Your market share erodes as competitors gain ground

This project tackles this head-on by building predictive models that can identify at-risk customers before they churn, enabling proactive retention strategies.

## What You'll Find Here

### 📊 Complete Analysis Workflow
- **Data Exploration**: Understanding customer behavior patterns
- **Feature Engineering**: Creating meaningful predictors from raw data
- **Model Building**: Three different machine learning approaches
- **Business Insights**: Actionable recommendations for retention

### 🤖 Machine Learning Models
- **Logistic Regression**: The reliable workhorse (79.9% accuracy)
- **Random Forest**: The ensemble champion (84.2% AUC)
- **Decision Tree**: The interpretable baseline

### 💡 Key Findings
- New customers (0-12 months) are 3x more likely to churn
- Month-to-month contracts drive 55% of all churn
- Higher monthly charges correlate strongly with attrition
- Electronic check users churn 20% more than others

## The Dataset

We're working with the Telco Customer Churn dataset from IBM, featuring:
- **7,043 customers** with complete service histories
- **21 features** covering demographics, services, and account details
- **26.5% churn rate** - a realistic business scenario
- Real-world data quality issues to handle

## Model Performance

Our best model achieves:
- **80% accuracy** in predicting churn
- **84% AUC score** for excellent discrimination
- **52% recall** for catching actual churners

But numbers only tell part of the story—the real value lies in the actionable insights that drive retention strategies.

## Business Impact

This analysis provides seven specific retention strategies with estimated impacts:
- **Enhanced onboarding** for new customers (15-20% churn reduction)
- **Contract incentives** for longer commitments (25-30% reduction)
- **Payment method optimization** (10-15% reduction)
- **Service bundling** improvements (12-18% reduction)
